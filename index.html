    What is this?

    Spotisaver is an independent Spotify playlist downloader and MP3 backup tool. Paste a Spotify playlist, album, or track link to read metadata, find matching audio from independent external sources where available, and create MP3 files or ZIP archives for personal use.

    Spotify links are used only to identify metadata such as track title, artist, album, cover art, release date, and playlist structure. Spotisaver does not download, rip, record, or access audio from Spotify, and it is not affiliated with Spotify AB.

    You can download individual matched tracks, create ZIP archives for playlists and albums, or export your track list to TXT. Users must only back up content they have the legal right to copy.

    Error: playlist is private?

    If you get a private playlist error, switch the playlist visibility to public and try loading it again.

    1. Open Spotify and go to the playlist page.

    After changing visibility, wait about a minute and click Load again.

    Spotisaver is not a Spotify ripper and does not download or record audio from Spotify. It uses user provided Spotify links only to identify metadata and search for matching audio from independent external sources where available.

    Why the playlist was not found or is unavailable.
    Why is the playlist not found or unavailable via the API?
    • Private playlist: Spotify API cannot access it.
    • Editorial (official) playlist: Such playlists are often unavailable due to API restrictions.
    How to fix this?
    1. If the playlist is private:
      Make it public:
      • Open Spotify on your computer.
      • Go to the desired playlist.
      • Click the three dots (…) and select Make Public.
    2. If the playlist is editorial:
      Copy tracks to your own new playlist:
      • Create a new playlist in Spotify.
      • Select all tracks in the editorial playlist by clicking the first track, then hold Shift and click the last track.
      • Right-click the selection, choose Add to Playlist → New Playlist.
      • Make your playlist public (see above).
    How to share the playlist link?

    To get the link to paste into the service:

    1. Open the desired playlist in Spotify.
    2. Click the three dots (…) next to the playlist name.
    3. Choose Share → Copy Playlist Link.
    4. Paste this link into the input field on the site.

    After that, the service will be able to process your playlist.
